MyVanilla: Loaded with Possibilities

Reloadable MyVanilla Prepaid Card is a simple, secure and convenient way to manage your money. No credit checks* to go through, no paper forms to fill. The MyVanilla Card can be used everywhere Visa debit cards or Debit MasterCard is accepted.

*This is not a credit card.

  • Add funds to your MyVanilla Card easily with Direct Deposit and Vanilla Reload.
  • Protect your cash with Visa Zero Liability† or MasterCard Zero Liability** Policy.
  • Stay connected to your account with the MyVanilla mobile app.
  • Set text alerts†† to stay updated on account balances and transactions.
